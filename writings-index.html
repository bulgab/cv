Bulga's writings in Mongolian language

I have a few writings in my mother tongue in my early twenties.
Didn't write anything in my thirties because I don't have much time for literature anymore.
Still I cherish the sentimental side of the human nature,
and would not want to lose my youthful writings,so I decided to put them up here.
These are purely amateur level writings, so I beg you be patient with them. ☺

I adored my dad.

I adored my dad. For he was the one to answer The inquisitive why questions of my earliest years He would point to the night sky And name the constellations. He showed me the little star Mitzar While my eyes could still see it That’s how I am one of those few Who have ever seen it I adored my daddy. For he was the first person to speak English to me My baby daddy was a better speaker than me Then he became unable To compete my English with time But my baby daddy, nobody is able to win my love over you And till my last gasp of breath You will stay alive in my heart I adored my daddy. For he always found my bloodstained underwear and wash them I proved grown up enough to receive menstruation But still childish enough not to do the laundry And my baby daddy proved motherly enough To wash the underwear of his long grown daughter I adored my daddy. For I would always wake up to see the breakfast readymade I adored my daddy. For I used to see him on all fours Cleaning the floor with all force He would perspire all over, by the toil My love would be inspired forever, by the sight I adored my daddy.

Бяцлав няцлав зурх сэтгэлийг. Буцаж ирэх тавилантай бурхан байв уу? Бузар орчлонгийн тоглоом энэ уу? Буянт аавыг минь булаан авав уу? Бэлгийн бурхныг буцаан шиднэ уу? Бурхан эргэж ирмэгц амьдрал эргэв. Бодол оорчлогдож дурлал хулжив. Ачилж амжаагуй аавыгаа л санана. Амьд улдсэн ээжийгээ л хайрлана! Эцгийг минь орлож ирсэн бурхныг Энгэртээ зуугээд инээмсэглээд Гунигаа нуугаад ээжээ хайрлаад Аавын охин амьдарч байна. Аавын охин амьсгалж байна!
